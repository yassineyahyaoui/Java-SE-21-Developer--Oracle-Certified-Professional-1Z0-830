### Q: What are the main exam objective areas for 1Z0-830?
Handling date/time/text/numeric/boolean; controlling program flow; Java object-oriented concepts; handling exceptions; arrays and collections; Lambda and Stream API; concurrency; I/O; and related topics per the official exam outline.

### Q: What should you do in the final review before the exam?
Flash-review all exam objectives; re-do previously missed questions; skim Java 21 additions (sealed types, records, pattern matching, virtual threads). Check logistics (ID, location, time) and get adequate rest.

### Q: Why re-do previously missed questions?
To confirm you now understand the concepts and can answer without hesitation. It reduces exam-day anxiety and catches any remaining gaps.
