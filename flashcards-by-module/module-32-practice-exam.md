# Module 32 -- Practice Exam

### Q: What score should you target on the practice exam?
Score at least 70% to demonstrate readiness. The real exam requires 68% (34 of 50); aiming higher on practice builds confidence and margin.

### Q: What should you do for each incorrect answer?
Understand why the correct answer is correct; write a small code snippet to verify; re-read the relevant exam objective. Do not move on until you can explain the concept.

### Q: What Java 21 topics should you verify before the exam?
Sealed types, records, pattern matching (instanceof and switch), and virtual threads. These are key additions on the 1Z0-830 and appear in many questions.
